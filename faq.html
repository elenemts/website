Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Home
  2. FAQ

1. What services do you offer?

We specialize in import and export solutions, including product sourcing, documentation, shipping logistics, and customs clearance.

2. Which countries do you operate in?

We currently operate across major international trade routes, with strong networks in Asia, Europe, North America, and the Middle East.

3. Can I request a custom quote?

Absolutely! Visit our Contact page or email us with your product details, quantity, and destination, and we’ll get back to you within 24 hours.

4. What industries do you serve?

We work with a wide range of sectors including agriculture, textiles, electronics, consumer goods, and more.

5. How do you ensure product quality?

We conduct supplier verification, quality inspections, and compliance checks to ensure all products meet international standards.

6. What are your payment terms?

Our standard terms include advance payments or LC (Letter of Credit), depending on the deal and region. Specifics are discussed case-by-case.

7. Do you handle shipping and logistics?

Yes, we offer complete end-to-end logistics support—from pickup to delivery, including customs handling.
